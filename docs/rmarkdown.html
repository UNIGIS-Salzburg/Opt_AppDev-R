Lesson 9 RMarkdown

This lesson is dedicated to the library rmarkdown. In fact, however, Rmarkdown is more than only a library. RMarkdown belongs to a set of tools that are designed to improve the reproducibility of your work. Other tools and platforms such as GitHub, Jupyter, Docker or preprint servers such as ArXiv or bioRxiv facilitate reproducibility in many different ways. In this module, we will not cover the full paradigm of reproducible research. Instead, we will focus on those aspects that will helping you make your analyses and reports more appealing, interactive and efficient.

In this lesson, we will weave together code and text in professionally rendered Rmarkdown documents. And we will use GitHub to savely store, share and administer our work.
